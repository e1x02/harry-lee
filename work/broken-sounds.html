Broken Sounds

Musical Toy, March 2015

A curious object for playful discovery and musical composition, Broken Sounds is part instrument, part performance. By moving the controller in different ways, players explore and interact with surprising soundscapes: a jack-in-the-box, a celestial radio, a telephone call, a living body, a theatre piece, a noise machine.

Created with Amani Naseem and Harrison Smith.
