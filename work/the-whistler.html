The Whistler

Live Game, December 2013

A mysterious transmission from an old radio. Shadows that chase you into the darkness. The far away sound of a haunting whistle, getting nearer. Will you survive the night? The Whistler is a live horror game where you sneak, uncover, run, hide, and cooperate to bind a supernatural evil.
