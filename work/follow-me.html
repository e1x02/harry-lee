Follow Me

Citywide Installation and Live Experience, August–October 2015

A series of stories where you literally follow the narrative by navigating city streets and making unusual connections.

You’re walking through the city, and you happen to pass a spot that makes you remember a specific time. Your first kiss with your high-school boyfriend next to that newsagent, or your first big trip to the city as a kid when you had lunch in that park. With Follow Me, we’re suggesting that it’s the city that does the remembering for us. For a limited time, a network of stories will leak out of the surfaces of the city, centred around the intersection of Elizabeth & Victoria Streets, giving us just a taste of all the things these walls we pass each day could tell us if we’d listen.

Presented as part of Melbourne Fringe 2015's Uncommon Places. Created with Georgia Symons.
